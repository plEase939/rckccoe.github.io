Milind Chandwani

Social Activist, Founder of NGO 'Camp Diaries', MTV Roadies Real Heroes (2019)

MILIND CHANDWANI

Milind Chandwani is an IT professional-turned-social activist. He has worked as a Senior Software Engineer at Infosys. After which he joined the NGO ‘Teach For India’ as a fellow, and then joined 'iTeach Schools' in Pune as a School Leader/Principal. Milind, who is a founder of an NGO 'Camp Diaries', works actively for the betterment and education of kids. He was also invited on the television reality show, 'MTV Roadies Real Heroes' as a Real Hero and 'Zee Heroes' as a celebrity contestant.

Rotaract Club of KC College of Engineering gives you the opportunity to serve on local, national and international level while working on projects ranging from professional development, skills, development, disaster relief, sanitation and hygiene, enpowering communities and promoting peace.
