About Us

RCKCCOE was chartered in the year 2017. Since then we have had 335+ Rotaractors till now. We at Rotaract, complete various projects based on avenues that not only increase the growth of an individual as a professional but also as a human being. Since 2017, we have completed 350+ such projects. Alone we can do so little, together we can achieve something marvelous. For the past 4 years, we have been working together in this Rotaract movement and this marks our fifth successful year! If you are dedicated to making a change in your community, we welcome you to RCKCCOE.

Why Rotaract?

1
We serve the community and hence, contribute to international peace.
2

You will be discovering some hidden potentials which are yet to explore inside you after engaging in social, professional, and community events.

Last but not least, we nurture change and benefit the community.

NEXT THINK TANK

NEXT Think Tank is a flagship project done annually under the Professional Development Avenue of RC KCCOE. Keeping the pandemic situation in mind and for everyone’s safety purposes RC KCCOE decided to conduct the event online. The theme chosen for the event was 3 Cs of Life – Choices, Chances & Changes which implied to “You must make a choice to take a chance, for your life to change.” And the guest speakers spoke the same. The speakers for the event were five unique personalities such as Milind Chandwani, Rashmi Chadha, Ajay Chopra, Prakruti Mishra, and Abhishek Hajela. The cause for NEXT Think Tank 2021 was Animal Welfare, for which RC KCCOE decided to collaborate with the Nonprofit Organization - Nature's Ally Foundation and vaccinate the stray dogs in the Thane area. Those who attended NEXT THINK TANK really got to learn something new, they got motivated and inspired to change their thinking and perspective towards life.

PRERNOTSAV

Prernotsav was conducted on Instagram LIVE where the speakers' account was connected with the official @rckccoe account and a LIVE interview was conducted. The speakers who were interested were SUYASH JADHAV - A Paralympian Swimmer and Arjuna Awardee, ANJUM MOUDGIL - An Olympian Rifle Shooter, and Arjuna Awardee, TUSHAR KHANDKER - An Olympian Hockey Player, RINKU HOODA - A Paralympic Javelin Thrower, AMIT HOODA - A Professional Kabaddi Player, TARUNDEEP RAI - An Olympian Archer, Arjuna Awardee, and Padma Shree Awardee and KHYATI GULANI - Former Cricketer of Indian Team and a Coach. The interview was pretty smooth where both the interviewer and guest had a friendly interaction. Followed by a rapid fire question & answer session was conducted and the guest answered them beautifully. The interview had an amazing audience from the speaker's side as well which included their friends, their sports mates, and other well-known and budding sportspersons making the whole event more lively.

Pride Is Every Month

Pride month is currently celebrated each year in the month of June to honor the 1969 Stonewall Uprising in Manhattan. Our aim of this project was that pride should not be celebrated in only June month but the whole year. The video from the speakers consisted of them telling about their coming out journey, their breaking point, how pride should be every month and should not be limited to one single month. There were a total of 19 speakers in the video, who described and beautifully presented the LGBTQ+ Community.
