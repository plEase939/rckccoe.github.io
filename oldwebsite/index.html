kanya

Community Service

Kanya was a magnanimous initiative by RC KCCOE wherein we donated sanitary napkins and spread awareness about menstruation and menstrual hygiene among the under-privileged girls of Kamilini Vidyalaya in Thane. There were 400+ sanitary napkins collected. This donation provided girls pads that might easily last at least for 5 months. The project was successful on a very large scale.

  • Date: 29th January, 2018
  • Project Chair Persons
  • Rtr. Nandini Dhavale

Sanskriti

International Service

When we say its incredible, we say its India. A country which is filled with culture, authenticity and love. Our mother language is a very rich way to represent our culture, tradition, state, place of birth and many more things. It is not just the matter of speaking a language or learning it but a beauty of expression. International Mother Language Day was celebrated on 21st February, 2018 to promote awareness of linguistic and cultural diversity and promote multilingualism. The project aimed at every state's Rotaract Club which took part in creating a video in which the rotaractors beautifully described their culture, spoke few phrases in their own mother language and also dressed in their traditional dresses. Sitting this far from all, everyone could connect to them, and an immense feeling of pride and patriotism was boosted within each one of us.

  • Date: 21st February, 2018
  • Project Chair Persons
  • Rtr. Nidhi Parihar & Rtr. Mahima Chawla

pride is every month

Community Service

LGBTQ+ is an important part of our ever growing society which contributes to the well-being and happiness of others. The most important thing we can learn from this community is how they love themselves even after being rejected by society's narrow mentality. Pride is Every Month video highlights everything that you should know about the LGBTQ+ community. Specially the "+" part, since people are still not very well acquainted by it. The main aim of the video was to make the society aware about the wide spectrum of genders so that they can get more open towards it.

  • Date: 23rd July, 2020
  • Project Chair Persons
  • Rtr. Anuja Badgujar & Rtr. Siddhi Ghosalkar.
